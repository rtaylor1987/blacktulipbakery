    About Us

    Hello. Nice to meet you.

    Tessa is an O’Neill local that moved back recently after spending a few years in Kansas City where she baked cakes on the side of a 9-5 insurance claims job. Her true passion lies with creating culinary masterpieces, though. She has a 12 year old son, Kaedyn. He is genuinely one of the best and good-hearted human beings ever.

    Grace is a life long Holt County local. She grew up in Chambers but has lived in O’Neill for about 10 years. She works as a nurse at both the local hospital and the elementary school (as her daughter’s para/nurse). She has a farming husband, Jess, and a nearly 5 year old daughter, Autumn. She’s a princess loving, hard rock jamming, spunky and sassy little lady.

    Tessa and Grace both parent children with differing special needs. Kaedyn was diagnosed with Autism when he was 4 years old and, thanks to Tessa’s advocation, has had exceptional care and resources and is doing very well. Autumn was diagnosed with Spinal Muscular Atrophy (a progressive weak muscle disease) at 4 months old and has been in a clinical trial for a few years. They are both thriving and loving kiddos who are their mama’s entire worlds.
